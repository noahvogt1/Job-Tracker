Privacy Policy

How Job Tracker handles your data.

1. What We Store

Job Tracker stores the data you enter into the application, such as jobs, applications, notes, offers, and basic account information (e.g., username and optional email address). This information is used solely to power the features of the app.

2. Authentication & Security

Passwords are hashed using industry‑standard algorithms and are never stored in plain text. Session tokens are used to keep you signed in and will move toward secure, httpOnly cookies for production deployments.

3. Use of Your Data

Your data is used to render dashboards, analytics, and notifications within Job Tracker. We do not sell your data. If integrations with email, calendars, or external services are enabled, they will be clearly documented and optional.

4. Analytics & Logging

Basic application logs may be kept to troubleshoot issues and improve reliability. These logs may include technical information such as timestamps, error messages, and API usage patterns, but are not used to build marketing profiles.

5. Your Choices

  • You can update or delete most records directly from the UI.
  • You can export data (when implemented) to maintain your own backups.
  • You can contact the maintainer to request account removal in self‑hosted or shared deployments.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may change as Job Tracker evolves. When it does, the “Last updated” date will be revised and, for significant changes, you may be asked to review the new policy.
